Kiwi Vision

The Flow Party is a safe, inclusive, and fun space for website developers and designers. We are an upbeat community, not for the faint of heart. We work hard but always in a Party Mood. Whether we're coding up a storm or taking a break to chat and share ideas, we always have a good time.

Challenges

Our goal is to keep on teaching and supporting the Community, and because Webflow has given us so much, we want to give back. We found a way to do both through our Challenges. Teach newbies, give pros an opportunity to showcase their work and help non-profits that make a difference in our world. while we're doing it.
‍
We choose a non-profit or a random idea to design and build a website and ask for mentors and community members to join us in a fun challenge. After everyone receives the briefing, assets and a timeframe is given, the competition begins. Mentors help guide and teach the participants throughout the process while the competition aspect keeps things lively. In the end A big live reveal event is scheduled with public voting and presentation to the client.
‍
The big reveal for Allissa's Animal Sanctuary will be on November 5th to a live audience of 3.9 million people! The winner of the challenge will also receive a prestigious "Digital CodeFish Award" - but that's a story for later... Make sure to tune in and see who comes out on top!

After Party

Well, first of all, they aren’t recorded. It’s a safe space for members to hang out, vent off or have tons of fun. We socialize, share tools, help, and support each other. It’s done after a Flow Party and allows us to chill after a working week while getting to know the other members. It’s quite like a Secret society, only... not so much! Because of its informality, it’s based on trust and friendship. Consider it a refuge for all the stressed creatives out there.The First Rule of The After Party Is: You do not talk about the After Party. The Second Rule of the After Party Is: you do not talk about the After Party.
